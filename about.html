About Bharat Yatra

Why this project exists, and how it was built.

Our Mission

To make India's destinations and cultural heritage easy to discover, plan for, and appreciate — through a fast, accessible, and genuinely useful web experience, built without unnecessary complexity.

Our Vision

A future where every traveller, student, and curious mind can explore heritage sites, plan meaningful trips, and understand living traditions — all from one place.
